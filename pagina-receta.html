Brazilian Lemonade


Brazilian lemonade is a drink that is tangy, creamy, and refreshing! It’s the perfect mix of tart and sweet and will have your family hooked after first sip.

"It's super easy to make! It’s also great for making big batches of. It’s a potluck favorite! Just a few minutes is all it takes to blend the ingredients together and you have a perfect, refreshing, summer lemonade…. or limeade."


Ingredients

  • 2 Limes
  • 3 Cups of water
  • 1/2 Cup of Sugar
  • 3 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 Cup Ice Cubes

Instructions

  1. Wash Limes thoroughly
  2. Cut off the ends and the inner white part of the lime
  3. Slice the lemon into eight wedges
  4. Place limes in blender with water, sugar, sweetened condensed milk and desired ice
  5. Blend the Ingredients for 30 seconds and pulse if necessary
  6. Strain through a fine mesh strainer
  7. Serve over ice
  8. Enjoy!
